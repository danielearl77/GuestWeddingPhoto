Welcome to Guest Wedding Photo

The website for your guests to share photos of your special day

Do you want copies of your friends and families photos of your wedding?

Are you thinking of putting disposable cameras on each table at your wedding?

If the answer is yes then this site is for you.

Guest Wedding Photo was born out of the desire at our own wedding to see how our friends and family saw our day. Disposable cameras on each table didn't really fit the style of our wedding, but we still wanted copies of our friends photographs. So, we built a basic site and put some cards on each table with the web address so everyone could upload their photos for us to see when we got back from honeymoon. That basic site has become what you see today.

All you need to do is sign up for a free account, create cards with www.guestweddingphoto.com and your unique PIN, place the cards on the tables at your wedding, and wait for the photos to be uploaded.

Help Signing Up?

All we need is your name, and your email address (which will become your username allowing you to log into the site). Once you have signed up you will be given your unique wedding PIN that your guests will use to upload their photos after your wedding. Once you have signed up and logged in for the first time you can provide us your wedding date, change your password, and update your name or email address if required.

If you have having trouble signing up please make sure you are filling in all the boxes on the form, with your name (first name and last name), email address, and password. Also make sure you have ticked the box agreeing to our terms of use. Our terms of use can be found by clicking on the terms of use link next to the tick-box or clicking on the terms and conditions link at the bottom of this page.

If you have any questions or problems please get in touch with us using the contact form at the bottom of this page.

Help Uploading Photos?

Your photos should be in one of the following formats .png .jpeg .jpg or .gif. The file size of the photos must not exceed 10mb per photo.

If you have taken your photos using a digital camera or mobile phone they should meet these requirements without you having to do anything else. If your internet connection is slow or unreliable that can also cause problems uploading photos.

Please also check that you are using the correct wedding PIN, as provided at the wedding.

If your photos are the correct size and format, your internet connection is good, and the PIN is correct but your photos still won’t upload then please contact us using the contact form and we will look into it for you.
